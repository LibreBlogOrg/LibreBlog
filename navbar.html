Navigation Add Navbar Item
Support notes
Add links to the header or footer of your website. You can create links to internal elements (sections, series, authors or articles) or you can insert an external link.

P.S. 1: There cannot be more than one item with the same Position in the same page component.

P.S. 2: There is a special type of navbar item called a label. It does not refer to any page, it is just used to subdivide your list of links into sections.
